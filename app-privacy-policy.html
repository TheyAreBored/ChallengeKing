Privacy Policy for Challenge King App

Effective Date: February 2025

1. Data Collection

The Challenge King app collects only non-personally identifiable information (Non-PII) directly. This includes anonymous usage data used solely for app performance monitoring and functionality improvement.

RevenueCat, a third-party service, is used for in-app purchase validation. RevenueCat only collects transactional data and anonymized device data to validate transactions and prevent fraud. This data is not used for tracking, profiling, or advertising purposes. RevenueCat does not collect any personally identifiable information such as email addresses or user names.

2. No Advertising or Marketing Data Sharing

The App does not display advertisements and does not share any data with other organizations for marketing purposes. Your information is only used to improve app functionality and process transactions securely.

3. Data Security

Non-PII collected by the Challenge King app and transactional data processed by RevenueCat are handled securely and in compliance with relevant privacy policies. No personally identifiable information is stored or shared by the app.

4. Children’s Privacy & Safety

The App is designed for children to play alongside a responsible adult. We do not knowingly collect or store personal information from children under 13. Parents and guardians are encouraged to monitor app usage and ensure their child’s safety in alignment with our Safety Policy, which can be found within the app’s settings.

5. User Rights

Users who wish to manage their data collected by third-party services (e.g., RevenueCat) can review and adjust their privacy settings directly through those platforms.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We encourage users to review this policy periodically to stay informed.

If you need support, feel free to contact us at team@challengeking.app
